2024 Whishlist

Bonfire 1.0

Our primary objective is the rollout of the first Release Candidate of Bonfire 1.0, a significant milestone in our journey. Upon release, we will engage actively with our community to identify and resolve any emerging bugs and potential optimisations.

iOS and Android app

We will harness the power of LiveViewNative, an amazing library developed by Dockyard, to create native iOS (shortly followed by Android) applications for Bonfire Classic. This advancement is crucial as it enables our communities to experience Bonfire natively, enhancing accessibility and user experience. Moreover, it sets a precedent for effortlessly porting future extensions to native platforms, significantly broadening our technological horizons.

Launch of Open Hosting Cooperative

Recognizing the challenges of hosting federated social platforms, our aim is to simplify this process. We plan to establish a premium hosting service, uniquely powered by cooperatives. This initiative is in line with our core values and is designed to strengthen the entire ecosystem, offering robust, value-driven hosting solutions.

Server and Infrastructure Optimization

Exploring cutting-edge server and infrastructure optimizations is on our roadmap, with a particular focus on technologies like FLAME and Supavisor. These innovations promise to enhance our platform's efficiency and scalability, reducing the cost and burden of scaling instances.

Creation of New Extensions

As we collaborate with various communities and pilot projects, our focus will expand to the development of bespoke extensions. Currently, we are channeling our efforts towards open science and community-oriented extensions. This is a strategic move to cater to the specific needs of our users and to enrich the functionality and versatility of the Bonfire platform.

Enhancement of Learning Resources

We understand the importance of accessible and comprehensive learning materials. Thus, a significant part of our agenda involves the creation of new tutorials and the enhancement of existing documentation. These resources are vital in empowering our users, fostering a deeper understanding of our platform, and facilitating a smoother user experience.

Final words

In summary, 2024 is poised to be a transformative year for Bonfire Networks, marked by the awaited 1.0 release, more community engagement, and a steadfast commitment to our core values and user needs. We fully recognize the ambitious nature of our goals outlined above. To bring these aspirations to reality, we are actively seeking collaboration and support. We are keen to expand our team by onboarding new developers and ensuring fair compensation for all contributors. And we want to keep having fun experimenting and pushing the boundaries of open digital spaces forward. If you want to participate and help make this a reality, don't hesitate to connect, Bonfire is an open collective and we're eager to hear from you.

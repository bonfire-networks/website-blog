Mosaic

Your online presence, unified

With Bonfire Mosaic, you partner with the Bonfire Team to seamlessly integrate your website, social network, and custom tools into a cohesive federated digital space.
Book a call

Yesterday, we used to work in companies.
Today, we thrive in networks.

Bonfire Mosaic empowers your project with...
  • A presentational website
    Custom designs
    Tailor your website's pages and posts to reflect your identity with flexible and customizable layouts.
    Privacy-friendly analytics
    Track engagement and performance with comprehensive metrics, all while maintaining visitor privacy—no more cookie banners required!
  • A federated social network
    Engagement galore
    Visitors can follow pages, authors, groups, and topics. Enable interactions like boosts, likes, and replies on content. All seamless for Fediverse users, with no extra sign-up!
    Trustworthy and safe
    You have tools and services to moderate your space to ensure it is a safe space for your community.
  • Ad-hoc tools & integrations
    Custom development
    Whether you need a custom features or to implement an existing one with a different user experience, that is tailored to your needs, the Bonfire modular framework allows for easy development.
    External integrations
    Integrate external tools and services that you already use into your Bonfire Mosaic instance to streamline your operations and save time. Your favourite tools are just one extension away.

Amplify your impact and productivity,
Reduce costs and complexity.

Unlike traditional websites or consumer social media platforms, Mosaic empowers you with standards-based federation to seamlessly connect with millions of users across a multitude of compatible apps.
  • Cost-effective unified platform
    Manage your website and social network from a single dashboard, consolidating multiple tools to streamline digital strategy and operations while significantly reducing development and maintenance expenses, and not being locked into an monthly subscription.
  • Meaningful engagement
    Build a vibrant community and expand your reach by seamlessly connecting with the Fediverse, fostering active participation and visibility.
  • Productivity without compromises
    Ensure the platform is accessible to anyone, with user-friendly design and standards-based features and APIs that support interoperability across various technologies.
  • Data sovereignty
    Choose self-hosting or our coop-hosting service to retain control over your data. Enable users to interact from anywhere while retaining ownership of their data.
  • Safety & privacy
    Implement robust features, policies, and governance tools to protect data, users, and interactions across the platform, prioritising safety and privacy at every level.

Bonfire Coordination

Bonfire Coordination is tailored for communities needing collaborative solutions, providing features for task assignment and progress tracking. With federated Kanban boards and task management, it enhances teamwork efficiency and empowers users. It uses the ValueFlows open economic vocabulary, which offers lots of potential to foster countless decentralised and cooperative community workflows.

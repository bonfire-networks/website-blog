Reach out

We want to partner with forward-looking communities to design and build tools together, and make them available for everyone to use, remix and experiment.

Do you want to discuss yours needs and see if a Bonfire app or extension might help? Do you want to contribute to building the Bonfire ecosystem? Want to get involved as a beta tester?

You can contact us by sending an email at team@bonfire.cafe or engaging with us on the fediverse.

To report abuse of our services or members of our community, please follow our code of conduct, which have more information about what to do.
